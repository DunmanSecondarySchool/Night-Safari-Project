Night Safari Visitors

Problem Statement

In 2020, the world's first nocturnal zoo, the Night safari in Singapore, attracted approximately 343 thousand
visitors. The Night Safari is part of the wildlife-centered leisure attractions in Singapore which includes the:

  • Singapore Zoo
    • Monkey
    • Zebra
    • Polar Bear
  • River Wonders
    • Parrot
    • Owl
    • Eagle
  • Jurong Bird Park
    • Panda
    • Red Panda

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the Night Safari was closed from April to June 2020,and has since then operated


at limited capacity under safety measures
